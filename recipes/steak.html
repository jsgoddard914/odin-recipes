Steak

This is my recipe for cooking a steak.

A good steak is one of my favorite meals. Hopefully after reading this recipe you will be able to make a good steak too.

Ingredients

    Salt
    Pepper
    Oil with High Smoke Point
    Preferred Cut of Steak

Instructions

    Pre-heat cast iron pan to 500 degrees.
    While your pan is heating up, pat your steak dry with a paper towel and add a thick layer of salt mixed with a little pepper to the steak on all sides.
    Once the pan is hot and your steak is seasoned, add a TB of oil to the pan.
    Immeadiately after adding your oil place the steak in your pan.
    Sear steak on all sides until a crispy, brown crust is achieved. About 2-3 minutes on each side.
    After searing let the steak rest on a plate for the same amount of time you spent cooking it, before you cut into it.
    Enjoy!
